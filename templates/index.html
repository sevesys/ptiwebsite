Who We Are

For over 30 years Panel Technologies has proudly served a wide range of industries providing a one-stop solution to our clients complex machining, manufacturing, and design needs.

Leveraging our experience in the industry and deep relationships with local suppliers we're able to consolidate multiple manufacturing needs and deliver complete metalwork to your doorstep.

We provide design, rapid prototyping, production, assembly, plating, painting, and silk-screening and work with a wide range of materials and part geometries. We work with industries such as data communications and telemetry, aviation, medical, defense, automotive, and transportation. Our customers range from innovators and local companies to the largest global corporations.
